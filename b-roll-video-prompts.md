## Usage Notes

- **Login Screen**: Use more dynamic, attention-grabbing videos (prompts 1-25) to create excitement and energy
- **Main Dashboard**: Use subtle, calming videos (prompts 26-50) that won't distract from the interface
- **Video Specifications**: Recommend 1920x1080 or 4K resolution, 30fps, 10-30 second loops
- **Opacity**: Consider using videos at 20-40% opacity as background overlays to maintain UI readability
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264) for best browser compatibility
- **Sources**: These prompts can be used with AI video generators (Runway, Pika, Stable Video) or stock footage sites (Artgrid, Storyblocks)
